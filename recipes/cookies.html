Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

Make the world's best, chewy and crispy chocolate chip cookie by following this easy recipe. This recipe by Dora, tested by Allrecipes Test Kitchen, will help you make 48 servings of cookies in just 5 simple steps!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanila
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • (Optional) Nuts

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
