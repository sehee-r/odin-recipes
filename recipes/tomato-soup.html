Tomato Soup

Description

Make a simple yet fresh tomato soup by following this quick recipe by Charlotte (via AllRecipes). This recipe will help you make the warm and delicious comfort food and bring you back to your childhood!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 large slice of onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.
  2. Run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl.
  3. Make a roux.
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season.
