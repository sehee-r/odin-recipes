Lasagna

Description

Make the world's best lasagna by following this easy recipe. This recipe by John Chandler will help you make 12 servings of delicious lasagna just under 3 hours. Feel free to customize any of the ingredients to suit your needs!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce, paste, or crushed tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
